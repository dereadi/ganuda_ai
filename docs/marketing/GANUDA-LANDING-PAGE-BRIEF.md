## Products Section

### VetAssist - Veterans Disability Claims Assistant

**Tagline:** Helping Veterans Get the Benefits They Earned

**Description:**
VetAssist is an AI-powered platform that guides veterans through the complex VA disability claims process. Built by veterans who understand the struggle, VetAssist provides:

- **Smart Form Wizard** - Step-by-step guidance for VA forms (21-526EZ, 21-0995, 21-4138)
- **VA Rating Calculator** - Accurate combined disability rating calculations
- **Evidence Checklist** - Know exactly what documentation you need
- **Condition Database** - 800+ service-connected conditions with CFR criteria
- **AI Chat Support** - Get answers to your claims questions 24/7

**Security Highlight:** Your medical records and personal information are protected with military-grade encryption. We never sell or share veteran data.

**Link:** vetassist.ganuda.us

### Tribal Vision - Intelligent Video & Audio Processing

**Tagline:** See More. Understand More. Protect More.

Tribal Vision brings enterprise-grade AI to video and audio processing:
