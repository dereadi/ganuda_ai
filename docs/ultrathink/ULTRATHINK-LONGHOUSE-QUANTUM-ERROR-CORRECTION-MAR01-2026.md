### The Coyote Insight

In QEC, **syndrome qubits** exist solely to detect errors. They don't carry information — they carry error signals.

Coyote's entire role is to detect errors. His `[DISSENT]` flag is not opposition. It is **measurement**. He measures whether the council's state has drifted outside the valid subspace. His dissent carries 2x weight in confidence calculation — exactly like a syndrome measurement that triggers correction.

Coyote is not the adversary. Coyote is the syndrome qubit.

### The Grandmother as Stabilizer

In stabilizer codes, a set of operators defines the valid subspace. Any state that violates a stabilizer gets projected back.

The grandmother veto (ghigau) defines the constitutional subspace:
- No weapons in the council house
- Seven-generation thinking required
- Sacred patterns cannot be overwritten
- Self-replication must be contained

A proposal that violates these doesn't get destroyed — it gets **projected back** into the valid subspace. The Longhouse defers, the proposer reframes, consensus re-attempts. This IS stabilizer correction.
