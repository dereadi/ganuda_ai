## Key Concepts

### Schrödinger Token
Token exists in **superposition of semantic states** until "observed" (output generated). Multiple potential meanings collapse to single interpretation during forward pass.

**Cherokee AI Parallel:** Thermal memory entries exist in superposition until accessed - high temperature = many potential interpretations, crystallization = collapse to fixed meaning.

### Frankenstein Vector
Combining **disparate reasoning paths** from different model components into unified output vector. Named for stitching together different "parts" of reasoning.

**Cherokee AI Parallel:** Council consensus synthesis - 7 specialists with different reasoning paths merged into single recommendation.

### Quantum Collapse
Multiple **parallel reasoning branches** evaluated simultaneously, then "collapsed" to single output based on confidence/relevance weighting.

**Cherokee AI Parallel:** Two Wolves pattern - Privacy and Security wolves reason in parallel, then collapse to synthesized wisdom.

### Multi-Parallel Realities
Transformer attention enables **concurrent evaluation** of multiple interpretation paths - not sequential like traditional computing.

**Cherokee AI Parallel:** Council specialists evaluate simultaneously, not sequentially.
