## Paper 1: Riemannian Liquid Spatio-Temporal Graph Network (RLSTG)

**Authors:** Lu, Wang, Dai, Liu, Shi (Guangdong U, Peking U, Sun Yat-Sen U)

### Problem
To understand causality in complex systems (traffic, biology, robotics), we must abandon "turn-based games" of discrete steps.

### Solution
- **Continuous Dynamics on Curved Manifolds**
- Force AI to solve differential equations on Riemannian surfaces
- Mathematically constrain the model to respect topological "shape" of data

### Outcome
- Stops hallucinating impossible trajectories
- Understands HOW change happens, not just THAT it happened

## Paper 2: Are LLMs Smarter Than Chimpanzees?

**Authors:** Yang, Zhao, Li, Li, Li (NTU Singapore, USTC, China Mining U)

### Problem
LLMs lack **epistemic isolation** - the ability to separate:
- Objective Truth vs Subjective Belief
- What the model knows vs what it should act on

### Root Cause
Attention mechanism connects "everything to everything" - LLMs act as "telepathic" entities.

### Consequence
- Savant at correlation
- Failure at strategy, negotiation, security
- Cannot model Theory of Mind (ToM)

### Cherokee AI Relevance
Our **7-Specialist Council** architecture addresses this:
- Each specialist has isolated concerns (SECURITY, 7GEN, PERF, etc.)
- Peace Chief synthesizes without telepathic access to all reasoning
- **Query Triad Interface**: User sees synthesis, full reasoning logged separately
