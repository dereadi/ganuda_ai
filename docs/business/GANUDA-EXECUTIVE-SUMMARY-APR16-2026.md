## The Opportunity

**Every organization deploying AI faces the same problem:** the model that generates a response is the same model judging whether that response is safe. There is no structural oversight, no separation of powers, no audit trail. The industry's answer — "make the model more aligned" — is equivalent to asking a witness to also serve as judge and jury.

Meanwhile, **$65B+ in annual AI spending** (by 2028) sits in compliance-restricted environments — DoD, HIPAA, financial, ITAR — that **cannot use frontier AI at all** because their data can't leave their network and local models can't match frontier capability.

---

## Our Solution

**Ganuda** is a constitutional governance layer for AI systems. Multiple independent AI models govern each other through democratic consensus, mandatory adversarial dissent, sycophancy detection, graduated autonomy tiers, and tamper-evident audit trails.
