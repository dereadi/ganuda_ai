## ðŸ”¬ HOW WE BUILT THIS

### Training Process:

**Phase 1: Cherokee Knowledge Injection**
- Corpus: 1.04 MB of Cherokee history, culture, language
- Base model: TinyLlama-1.1B (open-source)
- Training: 3 days on 3 GPUs
- Result: Model knows Cherokee facts and history

**Phase 2 Redux: Cherokee Behavioral Training**
- Corpus: 2,270 behavioral scenarios across 24 life domains
- Method: LoRA adapters (preserves Phase 1 knowledge)
- Training: 5 minutes, only 0.41% of parameters modified
- Result: Model gives Cherokee-values-based guidance

### Why LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)?
- Preserves all Phase 1 knowledge (no catastrophic forgetting)
- Only trains 4.5M of 1.1B parameters (efficient)
- Can be enabled/disabled (reversible)
- Faster training (5 minutes vs days)

### Cherokee Jr Consultation:
We consulted three AI "Jrs" (Council Jr, Trading Jr, Synthesis Jr) who guided the training approach:
- **Council Jr**: "Preserve Phase 1 knowledge while adding behavior"
- **Trading Jr**: "Higher learning rate, more data, fewer epochs"
- **Synthesis Jr**: "LoRA adapters prevent catastrophic forgetting"

All three were proven correct in testing.
