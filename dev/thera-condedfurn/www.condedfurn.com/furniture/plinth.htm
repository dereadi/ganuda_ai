Plinth

The central piece of CE furniture.. Made out of Ozark Red Oak. Plinths are 30" wide by 70" long. Roughly 20" tall. Precatalyzed lacquer covers a vinyl sealer to provide a very durable long lasting finish. At our local school there are furniture pieces that have been washed every school day for the last 4 1/2 years with a bleach solution and the finish is still holding up.

Shown with a grip bar, conductor's stool and a ladderback chair.
